Welcome to Taytay, Rizal

Welcome to Taytay, Rizal, a thriving town known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and booming garment industry. Whether you are a history buff, a shopping enthusiast, or a food lover, Taytay offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity that makes it a must-visit destination.


About Taytay

Taytay is widely recognized as the "Garments Capital of the Philippines", providing affordable yet fashionable clothing to consumers across the country. Beyond its economic success, the town is also home to historical landmarks, bustling markets, and a warm, tight-knit community that welcomes visitors with open arms.

History of Taytay

Founded in 1579, Taytay has a long and fascinating history that traces back to Spanish colonization, evident in its well-preserved churches and historical structures. Over the centuries, it evolved from an agricultural settlement into a major commercial hub, balancing progress with its deep cultural roots.

Come and Explore!

Taytay is not just about fashion—it’s a town filled with exciting experiences, from exploring centuries-old churches to indulging in delicious local street food like Epolag. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or a shopping spree at the famous Taytay Tiangge, this town has something special for everyone.



St. John the Baptist Parish

One of the oldest churches in Taytay, known for its historical and religious significance.

Taytay Tiangge

One of the largest garment markets in the country, making Taytay the "Garments Capital of the Philippines.

Ita's Epalog

A traditional Taytay dish, loved for its unique flavor and heritage.
The epalog is simply a hard-boiled egg encased in a batter that's deep-fried to a golden crisp..

Dolores Churc

A beautiful Catholic church that serves as a spiritual center for the community.
A perfect place for architecture lovers and those interested in local Catholic traditions..

Kalayaan Park

A historic landmark that honors Filipino heroes and offers a serene environment for relaxation.

Club Manila East

A popular resort known for its exciting water attractions and relaxing ambiance.
